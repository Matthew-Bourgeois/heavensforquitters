Our Mission

Sometimes it takes something drastic to finally get your attention. Most people call it “rock bottom”—that place where you feel like there’s nowhere left to go and no more reason to keep going. That’s where Jesus met me. Not at my best, but at my worst. And that’s where the idea for Heaven’s for Quitters was born—out of the journey of crawling out of a dark place and realizing the only way forward was surrender.

This brand isn’t about looking perfect. It’s about choosing a new direction—every single day. Each design is a reminder that we’re all fighting something, and that turning away from sin is a battle worth wearing.

Why I Quit Sin

“I spent years chasing approval, seeking comfort in hookups and attention, and numbing my pain with substances. I smiled on the outside but quietly struggled with anxiety and feeling unworthy. I hid behind distractions—money, image, and sin—trying to survive. But when I finally hit rock bottom and cried out to God, He didn’t turn away—He met me there. It wasn’t an overnight fix. Healing has come one surrendered day at a time. That’s where faith lives—in the moments you keep calling on Him, even when you feel like quitting. This shirt is more than fabric—it’s a reminder of what I left behind and the grace that carries me forward every day.”

— Founder, Heaven’s for Quitters
